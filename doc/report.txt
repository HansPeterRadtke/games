Important local-only rule
Do not commit:
- imported asset folders under `unity/projects/fps_demo/Assets/...`
- downloaded `.unitypackage` files
- local asset registry/generated metadata for imported third-party art

Known limitations at this checkpoint
- scaffold packages currently contain package structure and marker scripts, not full standalone demos yet
- `SceneBootstrap` and several editor tools are still in `com.hpr.fpsdemo`
- `Gameplay.unity` is the authoritative playable scene and has been updated to serialize the new runtime service bindings

Recommended next steps
